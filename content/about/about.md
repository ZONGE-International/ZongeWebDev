## Who We Are

Zonge International is an employee-owned geophysical services company and instrument manufacturer.
Founded by Dr. Ken Zonge in 1972, we focus on applying electrical, electromagnetic, and potential-field methods to image the Earth's subsurface.

Our goal is to provide state-of-the-art instruments to exploration groups worldwide and continue to be among the world's leading geophysical service providers. We look at each day as an opportunity to become a better company.

We are a true medium-sized company, with the nucleus of our field services existing in the Biggest Little City (Reno, NV). Our instrument manufacturing and second field office is Tucson, Arizona. We also have an office in Hermosillo, Mexico.
