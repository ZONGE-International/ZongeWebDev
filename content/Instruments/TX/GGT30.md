## Overview

The GGT-30B is a high-power broadband geophysical transmitter built for demanding time-domain and frequency-domain field programs. Its constant-current architecture, fast turn-off performance, and rugged modular package make it a strong choice for crews working with large loops or grounded dipoles in challenging environments.

The GGT-30B is especially well suited for customers already operating a ZMG-30 series generator package. For many new deployments, Zonge may instead recommend a [ZT-100B Geophysical Transmitter](/instruments/tx/zt100/) depending on the planned power system and survey configuration.

## Where It Fits

- Time-domain IP surveys
- Frequency-domain IP and complex resistivity work
- TEM programs
- FEM and CSAMT surveys
- Projects using loops or grounded dipoles

## Key Specifications

- Frequency range: `DC to 8 kHz`
- Output current range: `0.2 to 45 A`
- Output voltage range: `50 to 1000 V`
- Current stability: `+/-0.2%`
- Fast turn-off: `125 us` for a `300 x 300 m2` loop
- Resistive-load turn-off: under `10 us`
- Maximum input: `30 kVA` at `30 C`
- Size: `56 x 40.8 x 43.2 cm`
- Weight: `71.4 kg`
- Operating temperature: `-40 C to +55 C`

## Controls And Monitoring

The GGT-30B includes field-oriented controls and indicators for high-power operation:

- power on/off control
- transmit/reset switch
- continuously adjustable locking current control
- stepped voltage tap selection from `50 V` to `1000 V`
- analog input and output voltage meters
- digital operating display for current, turn-off time, transformer temperature, and input power

It also provides fault and status indication for conditions such as overcurrent, overvoltage, phase error, open circuit, and end-of-regulation events.

## Integration

Switching is controlled by an external controller. The specification sheet recommends pairing the transmitter with a Zonge `XMT`, `GDP`, or `ZEN` control system depending on the survey workflow.

Power is supplied from a three-phase `120 VAC`, `400 Hz +/- 15 Hz` source, with Zonge `ZMG` motor-generator sets recommended for field deployment.

## Options

- IGBT spare module
- SCR spare module
- `ZCM-10` isolated current monitor isolation amplifier module
- `LB6000` resistor load bank
- `ETS-9` switch box for rapid switching of dipole pairs

## Why Customers Choose It

The GGT-30B is the transmitter customers look to when they need proven high-power performance, fast loop turn-off, and a transmitter platform capable of supporting a wide range of EM and electrical survey methods from one rugged field unit.
