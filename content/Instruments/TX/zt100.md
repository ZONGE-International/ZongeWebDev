## Overview

The ZT-100B is Zonge's modern GPS-synchronized transmitter platform for advanced field operations in demanding survey conditions. It combines rugged field hardware, flexible operating modes, and a built-in web interface into a transmitter system designed for crews that want capability, control, and reliability in one package.

For customers building a new high-performance transmitter system, the ZT-100B is the premium Zonge option for teams that want a current-generation platform with modern control and workflow flexibility.

## Why It Stands Out

- GPS-synchronized transmitter control
- Local front-panel operation plus Wi-Fi browser control
- Modern field interface with dedicated `RESET` and `TRANSMIT` controls
- Support for standard and custom transmit sequences
- Built-in diagnostics, status reporting, and firmware management
- Designed for integration with Zonge power and controller workflows

## Where It Fits

- Time-domain transmission programs
- Frequency-domain field operations
- Projects needing repeatable synchronized control
- Crews that want both local and remote transmitter access
- Survey teams using Zonge generator and controller ecosystems

## Key Capabilities

- Operating modes: `100% DC`, `50%`, `Custom`, and `MMR 5Hz`
- Frequency selection from `0.0078125 Hz` up to `8192 Hz`
- `50%` mode limited to lower maximum frequency for that waveform family
- User-defined control signals through uploaded `.usm` files in `Custom` mode
- Wi-Fi web interface for control, logs, settings, and updates
- Front-panel transflective display with LED backlight for day and night use

## Hardware And Integration

The manual describes a field-ready control package with:

- `128 x 64` transflective display
- local control buttons for fast setup and adjustment
- dedicated `RESET` and `TRANSMIT` action buttons
- `15-pin` MIL-spec transmitter interface connector
- `3-pin` MIL-spec power connector
- `50 Ohm` BNC connector for active GPS antenna
- CAN output connector


## Buyer Benefits

The ZT-100B is attractive for customers who want more than a basic transmitter. It gives field crews a modern interface, synchronized timing, remote access, and custom waveform flexibility while staying inside the Zonge ecosystem. That means faster setup, cleaner integration, and a transmitter platform that feels current rather than improvised.
