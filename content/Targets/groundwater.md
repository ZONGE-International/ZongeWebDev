# Groundwater and Geophysics

Zonge offers advanced geophysical services for groundwater exploration using electrical, electromagnetic (EM), and gravity methods. These non-invasive techniques provide quick, accurate assessments of subsurface conditions, helping to locate and map water-bearing formations. Our methods are environmentally friendly and ideal for guiding efficient drilling strategies.
